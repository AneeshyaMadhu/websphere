Alumni Success Stories

Ankit Ashokan

IPS

"Ankit Asokan, a proud alumnus of Government Engineering College (GEC) Idukki from the 2007–2011 Information Technology batch, has carved an extraordinary path in public service. Known among peers and faculty for his unwavering dedication, sharp intellect, and natural leadership, Ankit transitioned from the world of technology to the prestigious Indian Police Service (IPS)—a journey that reflects both courage and commitment. Today, he serves in the Cyber Cell Division, where he plays a pivotal role in safeguarding digital infrastructure and combating the growing threat of cybercrime. His work involves not only technical investigation and threat mitigation but also public awareness and policy development to ensure a safer digital environment for all. Ankit’s story stands as a powerful testament to how a strong foundation in engineering and a passion for service can converge to create meaningful impact."

Anish Mohammad

"Anish Mohammad, a distinguished alumnus of Government Engineering College (GEC) Idukki, currently serves as the Group Chief Information Officer (CIO) of Lulu Group International—one of the world’s foremost retail conglomerates operating across 23 countries. With over 17 years of experience in Information Technology, Anish has become a driving force behind the group’s global digital transformation. His strategic leadership has been instrumental in launching and scaling Lulu’s e-commerce platforms, modernizing retail operations, and aligning technology initiatives with business growth. From his early days as a Junior Software Engineer to leading a team of over 1,500 IT professionals, Anish’s journey reflects a blend of technical brilliance, visionary thinking, and relentless dedication. His rise from the classrooms of GEC Idukki to the helm of international retail innovation continues to inspire students to dream big, embrace change, and pursue excellence in both technology and leadership."
